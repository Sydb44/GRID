# Encounter Factorum Outpost - Official Space Engineers Wiki

The Factorum Outpost is an [Encounter](https://spaceengineers.wiki.gg/wiki/Global_Encounters "Global Encounters") with [the Factorum](https://spaceengineers.wiki.gg/wiki/The_Factorum "The Factorum").

## Spoilerfree description

This Factorum outpost is very well hidden in an asteroid. When approaching the asteroid, you can see windows from two sides. Are they hiding anything valuable in the large hangar?

## Spoilers

**Spoiler warning!**  
This article or section contains details about the game which players may be intended to discover on their own!

The internal name for this outpost is ambush... but it has very valuable loot.

Outside, upstairs near the gate, there is a [Willis Duct](https://spaceengineers.wiki.gg/wiki/Willis_Ducts "Willis Ducts") to sneak in.

### Traps

*   Entrance armed with an assault cannon
*   Sneaky turrets inside
*   Three Warheads will count down to blow up the asteroid after you damage the first block, e.g. after you grind through doors
    *   After being detected by the security system, grind the warheads down fast to disarm the base...
*   At the same time, deal with the arrival of Drones outside...
